About This System

This prototype replicates the physical IF-AT scratch card experience for online learning. Teachers can create classes and quizzes, while students scratch to reveal answers and receive immediate feedback.

For Teachers

Create classes with join codes, build quizzes with detailed explanations, and access comprehensive learning analytics.

For Students

Join classes, take quizzes multiple times, and learn from immediate feedback with each scratch attempt.

Research Data

Detailed tracking of scratch events, timing data, and performance metrics for learning analytics research.
